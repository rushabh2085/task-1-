bloodhound

In a city ruled by silence and steel, Bloodhound walks the path of vengeance. Her blade knows no hesitation — only justice. When the night calls, she answers.

Feared in the underworld, whispered in the alleys — she is both hunter and hunted. Every scar tells a story, every strike brings her closer to the truth. In the war between shadows, Bloodhound is the blade that never breaks.
